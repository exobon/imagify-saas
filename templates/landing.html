Enhancer Frequently Asked Questions

What makes this proprietary engine different? +

Unlike generic bicubic filters or open-source upscalers that blur or add plastic artifacts, our proprietary AI restoration engine uses custom generative neural layers to hallucinate missing pixel details, textures, and lines in photographic and cartoon textures safely.

Will there be an API available? +

Yes. The Developer API package will grant direct REST endpoint access for programmatic requests. You can pass raw images or URLs and fetch enhanced, upscaled buffers directly in your software stack.

Are my uploaded photos secure and private? +

Absolutely. Privacy is our top priority. All uploaded assets are processed temporarily on secure private cloud GPUs and are automatically purged from our servers within 10 minutes after processing. Your images are never stored permanently or used to train public models.

How does the credit deduction system work? +

Each image generation request costs exactly 1 credit. Credits are deducted when you request the generation. If the API returns an error or fails to respond, the system will immediately refund the deducted credit back to your account balance.

How can I get more credits? +

All registered users receive 1 free credit to test the workshop. If you run out of credits, you can easily top up via WhatsApp support starting at just $5 (200 credits) by clicking the purchase buttons or messaging +919193987739.

Where are the generated images saved? +

To prevent broken link issues, our backend automatically downloads every successful generation and saves it locally in the project's static assets folder. They are permanently accessible in your workshop history gallery.
